Our Story

CoolComfort Air Solutions was founded with a simple mission: to provide homeowners and businesses in Selangor with air-con services they can truly trust. After years of seeing customers frustrated by unreliable technicians and surprise charges, we decided to build a company centered on professionalism, transparency, and technical expertise.

From our base in Petaling Jaya, we have grown a team of dedicated, certified professionals who share our commitment to excellence. We believe that a comfortable home starts with a reliable air-conditioner, and we're here to ensure yours runs perfectly.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted and sought-after air-conditioning service provider in Selangor by consistently delivering superior technical service, transparent communication, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients by ensuring their comfort and peace of mind.
