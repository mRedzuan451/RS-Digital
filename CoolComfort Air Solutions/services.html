Comprehensive Air-Conditioning Solutions

Whatever your air-con needs, our expert team has the skills and tools to get the job done right.

General Servicing

Our standard service is perfect for routine maintenance. It includes cleaning the indoor unit's filter and cover, checking the outdoor unit, clearing the drainage pipe, and testing the system to ensure optimal performance and energy efficiency.

Chemical Wash

For a deeper clean, our chemical wash service involves dismantling the indoor unit and using safe, specialized chemicals to thoroughly clean the internal components like the cooling coils and blower wheel. This removes stubborn mold and grime, improving air quality and cooling power.

Repair & Troubleshooting

Is your air-con not cold, leaking water, making strange noises, or not turning on? Our technicians are experts at diagnosing and fixing all common (and uncommon) problems quickly and effectively. We use quality replacement parts for lasting repairs.

New Unit Installation

We provide professional installation services for all major air-con brands. Our process ensures your new unit is installed correctly for maximum efficiency and longevity, with neat wiring and proper pipe routing. We'll help you choose the right unit for your space and budget.
